Patient ID

Prefix and Suffix is static, only the starting number increments

Once you’ve updated, prefix, suffix, and starting number, Kavanii will generate the patient id based on the last saved prefix, suffix, and starting number for all future transactions.

The patient Id of past transactions will remain intact. It's not mandatory to have a suffix, so you can leave the field blank if you don’t want one.
